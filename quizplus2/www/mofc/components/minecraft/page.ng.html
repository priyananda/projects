Instructions on crafting the Nether portal.

  1. Construct a 5x5 frame with Obsidian blocks.
  2. Fill the interior with Fire,Water and Lava blocks such that there is one more Fire block than Water, and one more Water block than Lava.
  3. Click on the block to cycle through different block types.
